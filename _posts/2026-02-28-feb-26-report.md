## Algiers Meetup


We organized Algiers Meetup, with 30+ attendees, 2 sessions and great energy. The theme was about applied mathematics in subjects of interest for the community.

IRL, we had a whiteboard session presented by [Rezazi Mohamed Abdessamed](https://www.linkedin.com/in/rezazi-mohamed-abdessamed/) from [Nethermind](https://www.nethermind.io/). It was an introduction to zero knowledge proofs.

![algiers meetup](/assets/images/algiers-meetup.jpeg)

Online, we had [Youssef El Housni](https://www.linkedin.com/in/youssef-el-housni-412427108/) from [Consensys](https://consensys.io/). It was about his career development from Math Olympiad in high school, to their [research](https://yelhousni.github.io/#publications) in applied cryptography securing millions of dollars on the blockchain.
